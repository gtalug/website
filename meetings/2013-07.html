meeting_title: "Mobile Web Apps" meeting_datetime: 2013-07-09T19:30:00 meeting_location: "George Vari Engineering and Computing Centre, Room 211" meeting_wiki_link: http://gtalug.org/wiki/Meetings:2013-08 meeting_speaker: "Myles Braithwaite" meeting_speaker_link: http://gtalug.org/wiki/User:Myles Back on 29th June, 2007, Apple completely revolutionized how we access data. At a time best most smartphones could offer was [WAP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wireless_Application_Protocol) they brought a mobile web browser that was comparable to the one on your desktop. In this presentation Myles would like to demonstrate that you can develop modern looking applications in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript without ever having to touch a line of Java or Object-C code and never have to be worried about getting rejected from an App Store.
